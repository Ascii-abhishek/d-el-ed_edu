Linear and Quadratic Equations

This chapter combines direct solving steps. Semester 4 papers ask both one-step linear equations and factor-based quadratic equations.

Fast Review

  • Linear example: \(3x = 4.5 \Rightarrow x = 1.5\)
  • Quadratic example: \(x^2 + x - 12 = 0\)
  • Factor form: \((x+4)(x-3)=0\)
  • So \(x = -4\) or \(x = 3\)

Tip

In short answers, write both steps and final roots clearly.
