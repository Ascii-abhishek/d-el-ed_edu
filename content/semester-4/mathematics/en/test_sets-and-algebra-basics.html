Section 2: Short Answer

1. Write \(A \cup B\) and \(A \cap B\) for \(A=\{1,3,5\}, B=\{3,4,5\}\).

\(A \cup B = \{1,3,4,5\}\) and \(A \cap B = \{3,5\}\).

2. Factorise \(x^2 + x - 12\).

\(x^2 + x - 12 = (x+4)(x-3)\)
