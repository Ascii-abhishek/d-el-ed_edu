Circle

Circle questions in Semester 4 are usually concept-based: parts of a circle, tangent facts, and simple angle observations.

Must-Know Terms

  • Radius: line from centre to circle
  • Diameter: longest chord, equal to \(2r\)
  • Chord: segment joining two points on circle
  • Tangent: touches circle at one point
  • Arc: part of circumference

Fast Fact

The tangent at any point of a circle is perpendicular to the radius through that point.
