Trigonometry

Scoring if standard values are memorised. Semester 4 mostly uses right-triangle ratios and simple substitutions.

Core Ratios

  • \(\sin \theta = \frac{\text{Perpendicular}}{\text{Hypotenuse}}\)
  • \(\cos \theta = \frac{\text{Base}}{\text{Hypotenuse}}\)
  • \(\tan \theta = \frac{\text{Perpendicular}}{\text{Base}}\)

Standard Values

  • \(\sin 30^\circ = \frac{1}{2}\)
  • \(\sin 60^\circ = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\)
  • \(\cos 30^\circ = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\)
  • \(\sec 60^\circ = 2\), \(\csc 30^\circ = 2\)

Exam questions often ask direct values like \(\frac{\sin 60^\circ}{\cos 30^\circ} - \sin 30^\circ\).
