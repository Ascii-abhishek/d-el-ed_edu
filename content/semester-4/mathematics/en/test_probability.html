Section 2: Short Answer

1. Write the sample space when two coins are tossed.

\(S = \{HH, HT, TH, TT\}\)

2. Find the probability of getting exactly one head in two coin tosses.

Favourable outcomes = HT, TH = 2

Total outcomes = 4

Probability \(= \frac{2}{4} = \frac{1}{2}\)
