Quadrilaterals

Important chapter for definitions, angle properties, and area questions. Trapezium and cyclic quadrilateral are especially useful for exams.

Quick List

  • Parallelogram: opposite sides parallel and equal
  • Rhombus: all sides equal
  • Rectangle: all angles \(90^\circ\)
  • Trapezium: one pair of opposite sides parallel
  • Cyclic quadrilateral: all vertices lie on a circle

Exam Fact

Opposite angles of a cyclic quadrilateral are supplementary.
