Section 2: Short Answer

1. Find the volume of a cylinder of radius 7 cm and height 10 cm.

\[ V = \pi r^2 h = \pi \times 7^2 \times 10 = 490\pi \]

Using \(\pi=\frac{22}{7}\), \(V = 1540 \text{ cm}^3\).

2. Find the area of a trapezium whose parallel sides are 25 cm and 12 cm, and height is 8 cm.

\[ A = \frac{1}{2}(25+12)\times 8 = \frac{1}{2}\times 37 \times 8 = 148 \text{ cm}^2 \]

Section 3: Long Answer

1. A rectangle 10 cm long and 7 cm broad is revolved around its length. Explain the solid formed and find its volume.

When the rectangle revolves around its length, it forms a cylinder.

  • Height \(h = 10\) cm
  • Radius \(r = 7\) cm

\[ V = \pi r^2 h = \pi \times 7^2 \times 10 = 490\pi \]

Using \(\pi=\frac{22}{7}\), volume \(= 1540 \text{ cm}^3\).
