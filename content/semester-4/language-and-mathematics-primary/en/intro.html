Language and Mathematics at Primary Level — Introduction

This subject focuses specifically on how language and mathematics are learned and taught in the earliest years of schooling (classes 1–5).

Key Areas Covered

  • Emergent literacy: oral language, print awareness, phonological awareness
  • Teaching reading: phonics, whole language, balanced approach
  • Early writing development and classroom strategies
  • Number sense and numeracy at the foundational stage
  • Concrete-Pictorial-Abstract (CPA) approach in mathematics
  • Common learning difficulties in language and maths

Why It Matters

The first three years of schooling set the trajectory for all future learning. How you teach language and maths in these years has lifelong impact.
