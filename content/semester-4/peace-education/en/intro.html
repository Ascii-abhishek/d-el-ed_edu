Peace Education and Sustainable Efforts — Introduction

This subject builds values of peace, harmony, and sustainability in future teachers, so they can nurture these same qualities in their students.

Key Areas Covered

  • Concept of peace: inner peace, social peace, global peace
  • Conflict resolution and non-violent communication
  • Education for sustainable development (ESD)
  • Environmental responsibility in school communities
  • Human rights and values education
  • Classroom activities that promote empathy and cooperation

Why It Matters

Primary teachers shape children's values in their most impressionable years. A classroom culture of peace and respect stays with children for life.
