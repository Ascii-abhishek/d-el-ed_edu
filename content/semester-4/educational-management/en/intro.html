Educational Management and Administration — Introduction

This subject covers how schools are managed, the administrative systems teachers operate within, and how to use resources effectively for student learning.

Key Areas Covered

  • Concept of educational management and administration
  • School management: headmaster's role, staff meetings, school records
  • School Management Committees (SMC) and community participation
  • Financial management: grants, Mid-Day Meal, school funds
  • Supervision and inspection of schools
  • Time-table planning and co-curricular activities

Why It Matters

Understanding how your school is managed helps you work effectively within the system and advocate for your students and classroom needs.
