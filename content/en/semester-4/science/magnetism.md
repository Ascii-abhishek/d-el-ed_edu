# Magnetism

This chapter explains magnets, magnetic materials, earth's magnetism, and simple electromagnets.

## Key Points

- A magnet has two poles: north and south.
- Like poles repel and unlike poles attract.
- Iron, nickel, and cobalt are common magnetic materials.
- Earth behaves like a large magnet.
- An electromagnet works when electric current flows through a coil.

## Classroom Connection

Let students test classroom objects with a magnet and classify them as magnetic or non-magnetic.
