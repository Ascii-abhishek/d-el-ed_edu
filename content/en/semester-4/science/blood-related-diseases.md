# Blood-related Diseases

This chapter gives a basic overview of common blood-related diseases and prevention habits.

## Key Points

- Anaemia is often linked with low haemoglobin.
- Some infections can spread through unsafe blood contact.
- AIDS and Hepatitis B require awareness and prevention.
- Clean needles and screened blood are important for safety.
- Early medical advice helps prevent complications.

## Classroom Connection

Discuss health awareness through posters, simple slogans, and community examples.
