# Reflective Journal

A reflective journal records teaching experiences and helps improve future lessons. It is more than a daily diary. It includes observation, analysis, and next steps.

## Suggested Format

1. What happened in class?
2. What went well?
3. What was difficult?
4. What did students understand?
5. What will I change next time?

## Practice

After every teaching session, write three short points while the experience is still fresh.
