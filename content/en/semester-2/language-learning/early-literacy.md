# Early Literacy

Early literacy begins before formal reading and writing. Children notice signs, listen to stories, identify sounds, hold books, draw symbols, and understand that print carries meaning.

## Classroom Ideas

- Read aloud daily.
- Keep picture books within reach.
- Use children's names for letter recognition.
- Let children draw and talk about their drawings.

## Important Reminder

Literacy grows through meaningful language use, not only through copying letters.
