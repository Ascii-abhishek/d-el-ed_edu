# Lesson Planning

A lesson plan is a simple guide for teaching. It helps the teacher decide what students should learn, which activities will support learning, and how understanding will be checked.

## Basic Parts

1. Learning objective.
2. Previous knowledge.
3. Teaching-learning materials.
4. Classroom activities.
5. Assessment and follow-up.

## Sample Use

Before teaching a poem, a teacher may begin with a picture discussion, read aloud with expression, ask children to repeat lines, and end with a drawing or oral response.
