# Meaning of Childhood

Childhood is a stage of life where children grow through play, relationships, language, curiosity, and social experience. For a teacher, childhood is not only a biological age group. It is also shaped by family, culture, community, school, and opportunities.

## Key Points

- Every child develops at a different pace.
- Play is an important way of learning.
- Children bring knowledge from home and community.
- Observation helps teachers understand children's needs.

## Classroom Connection

Notice how children ask questions, use local language, share stories, and solve small problems. These everyday actions give useful clues for planning lessons.

## Reflect

Write one example from a child you know that shows curiosity, imagination, or problem solving.
