Inclusive Education — Introduction

Inclusive education means that all children — regardless of disability, gender, caste, or background — are welcomed and supported in the same classroom.

Key Areas Covered

  • Concept and philosophy of inclusive education
  • Types of disabilities: physical, sensory, intellectual, learning
  • Legal framework: RTE Act, RPWD Act 2016
  • Identifying children with special needs in the classroom
  • Differentiated instruction and Universal Design for Learning (UDL)
  • Role of family and community in inclusive schooling

Classroom Connection

Small adjustments like flexible seating, peer support, and multiple ways to demonstrate learning go a long way toward inclusion.
