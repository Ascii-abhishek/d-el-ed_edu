Social Science — Introduction (Semester 1)

Social science at the primary level integrates history, geography, and civics to help children understand their society and surroundings.

Key Areas Covered

  • Family, community, and local neighbourhood
  • Maps and directions — our school, village, town
  • Food, clothing, and shelter across regions
  • Historical figures and national symbols
  • Democratic values and citizenship

Classroom Connection

Connect social science topics to children's own lives — their family, village, and local festivals make the best starting points.

Reflect

What local history or cultural practice from your area could you bring into a social science lesson?
