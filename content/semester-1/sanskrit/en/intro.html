Sanskrit — Introduction (Semester 1)

This subject introduces D.El.Ed students to basic Sanskrit language skills needed to teach at the primary level and understand classical texts.

Key Areas Covered

  • Sanskrit alphabet: swaras and vyanjanas
  • Basic nouns and verb forms (dhatus)
  • Simple sentences and translation
  • Short shlokas and their meaning
  • Cultural significance of Sanskrit in Indian education

Classroom Connection

Shlokas, counting, and simple greetings in Sanskrit make excellent classroom starters for primary children.
