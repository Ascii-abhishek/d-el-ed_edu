Child Development and Learning — Introduction

This subject explores how children grow, develop, and learn from birth through adolescence. It is the foundation of D.El.Ed and shapes how you approach every child in your classroom.

Key Areas Covered

  • Stages of physical, cognitive, social, and emotional development
  • Theories of learning: Piaget, Vygotsky, Bruner
  • Role of heredity and environment
  • Individual differences in children
  • Motivation and its role in learning

Why It Matters

Understanding child development helps you plan age-appropriate lessons, respond to classroom behaviour with empathy, and identify children who may need extra support.

Reflect

Think of one child you have observed — how did their home environment seem to affect their behaviour in school?
