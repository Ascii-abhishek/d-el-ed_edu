Hindi — Introduction (Semester 1)

This subject builds language skills in Hindi for D.El.Ed students and prepares them to teach Hindi effectively at the primary level.

Key Areas Covered

  • Hindi script: varnamala, matras, and conjunct letters
  • Grammar: nouns, pronouns, verbs, tenses
  • Reading comprehension and unseen passages
  • Writing: essays, letters, summaries
  • Teaching Hindi at primary level: methods and activities

Classroom Connection

Use stories, rhymes, and picture books to make Hindi enjoyable for young learners.
