Science — Introduction (Semester 1)

This subject builds your understanding of basic science concepts taught at the primary level and how to teach them effectively to young children.

Key Areas Covered

  • Nature and scope of science at primary level
  • Living and non-living things
  • Plants and animals — basic classification
  • Our body — organs and their functions
  • Water, air, and soil in daily life
  • Simple experiments for classroom use

Classroom Connection

Science at the primary level is best taught through observation and hands-on activities rather than textbook reading alone.
