Mathematics — Introduction (Semester 1)

This subject covers foundational mathematics concepts and the methods used to teach them at the primary level.

Key Areas Covered

  • Number sense and counting
  • Basic operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, division
  • Place value and the number system
  • Shapes and spatial understanding
  • Measurement: length, weight, time
  • Using concrete materials to teach maths

Classroom Connection

Young children learn mathematics best through concrete objects before moving to pictures and then abstract symbols.
