Principles of Teaching Learning — Introduction

This subject covers the core principles that guide how teaching and learning happen in classrooms. It connects learning theory to everyday teaching decisions.

Key Areas Covered

  • Principles of learning: readiness, exercise, effect
  • Teaching methods: lecture, demonstration, discussion, project
  • Lesson planning and instructional objectives
  • Relationship between teaching and learning
  • Role of feedback and reinforcement

Why It Matters

These principles help you choose the right teaching strategy for each topic and each group of children, making your lessons more effective.
