Contemporary Indian Society and Primary Education — Introduction

This subject examines the social structures, diversity, and challenges of modern India, and how primary education responds to these realities.

Key Areas Covered

  • Social diversity: caste, religion, language, and gender
  • Constitutional values: equality, justice, liberty, fraternity
  • Role of education in social change
  • Challenges: poverty, child labour, discrimination in schooling
  • Government schemes for primary education: SSA, RTE Act 2009

Why It Matters

A teacher who understands social context can build a more inclusive, sensitive, and equitable classroom.
