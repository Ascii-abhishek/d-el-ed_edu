New Efforts in Primary Education — Introduction

This subject covers recent reforms, policies, and innovative programmes that have shaped primary education in India over the past few decades.

Key Areas Covered

  • National Education Policy (NEP 2020) highlights
  • Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) and RMSA
  • Right to Education Act 2009: key provisions
  • Activity-based and child-centred learning innovations
  • Midday Meal Scheme and its impact on enrolment
  • Digital India and technology in classrooms

Why It Matters

Knowing recent policy helps you understand why certain practices are expected of teachers and what official support exists for your school.
